Privacy Policy

App: Lorebind (the "App") · Effective: June 17, 2026

Yuichiro Suzuki (the "Provider") sets out the following Privacy Policy regarding the handling of user information in the App.

1. Basic Policy

The App stores the data you create (worlds, characters, timelines, images, etc.) only on your device. It is never transmitted to or collected by the Provider's servers or any third party.

2. Information We Collect

The Provider does not collect any personal information through the App, such as your name, contact details, location, or contacts. We do not perform usage analytics or behavioral tracking, and we do not show ads.

3. Your Created Data

All text and images you create, enter, or attach in the App are stored only on your device (and in files you choose to export yourself). The Provider cannot view or obtain this content.

4. In-App Purchases

All purchases, billing, renewals, and cancellations of the paid plan (Lorebind Plus) are handled through Apple's App Store. Payment and credit card information is managed by Apple; the Provider does not receive it. Please see Apple's privacy policy for details.

5. Disclosure / External Transmission

The Provider does not provide your information to third parties. The App does not embed any external analytics or advertising services.

6. Children's Privacy

Because the App does not collect personal information, it can be used safely by people of any age.
