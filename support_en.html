Lorebind — Support

Lorebind is an offline worldbuilding and setting-management app for novelists, TTRPG game masters, and creators. This page provides support information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored only on your device. It is never sent to our servers. There are no ads and no tracking. (iCloud sync across devices is planned for a future update.)

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings > Data backup > Create backup, then save or share the file. To restore, use Settings > Data backup > Restore from backup. You can also export a world to Markdown (free) or PDF (Plus).

What does Lorebind Plus include?

Unlimited worlds, image attachments, the map view, and PDF export. Choose a monthly plan, a yearly plan (with a 7-day free trial), or a one-time purchase. The core features are free.

How do I manage or cancel a subscription?

Open your device's Settings > your Apple ID > Subscriptions, or use the link inside the app (Settings > Lorebind Plus). Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. After canceling, the worlds and images you've created remain available.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Open the paywall or Settings and tap "Restore Purchases" while signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
